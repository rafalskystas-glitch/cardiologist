What I Offer

Services

A full spectrum of cardiological care — from initial consultation to comprehensive diagnostics and treatment.

🫀
Cardiology Consultation

Diagnosis and management of cardiovascular conditions. Prevention of complications from hypertension, atherosclerosis, and myocardial inflammatory disease.

📊
ECG & Holter Monitoring

24-hour Holter ECG monitoring (Arnika, DiaCard, CardioSpy) and ambulatory blood pressure monitoring (ABPM) using CardioTens.

🔬
Echocardiography (Echo)

Independently performs echocardiography on Philips iE33, CX50, Toshiba Artida, GE Logiq 7, GE Versana Essential, and Toshiba Aplio 500.

🤰
Cardiology in Pregnancy

Specialised care for cardiovascular conditions during pregnancy. Cardiac monitoring and medication adjustment for expectant mothers.

💨
Spirometry

Assessment of pulmonary function using Spirobank MIR SPIRO — resting spirogram and post-bronchodilator testing.

😴
Sleep Study (Somnography)

Sleep disorder diagnostics with Somnocheck Micro; full polysomnography on Somnolab 2. Detection of apnoea and other sleep conditions.

About Me

Experience & Qualifications

For more than 16 years I have dedicated myself to cardiology, helping patients maintain an active and fulfilling life. My central goal is to do everything possible so that you never need a cardiac surgeon.

  • Internship in Internal Medicine, 2010–2012

  • Member of the European Association of Cardiovascular Imaging (EACVI)
  • Member of the Heart Failure Association (HFA ESC)
  • Medical Expert on Television
  • Regular participant in leading international cardiology conferences
